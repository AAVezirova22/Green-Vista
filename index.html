As you can see, the pollution in Bulgaria is very severe and we need to think about not only us, but the future generations. The consequences of air, water, and soil pollution in Bulgaria have been well-documented, and have caused harm to people's health, the local economy, and the environment. Therefore, we must take urgent action to ad dress this issue in order to ensure a safer and more sustainable future for ourselves and future generations.

How we met

Our first date was in high school. Little by little we got to know each other and found common topics of conversation. We started writing to each other and became friends.

How we decided to work together

It's interesting that we didn't decide to work together because we're friends. It all started after an excursion to Sofia, organized by our school. In Sofia, we visited several companies with software services. That's when our idea to work as a team came up.

Why we decided to create a website on this topic?
