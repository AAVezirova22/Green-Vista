Challenge yourself!


It's never too late to help. Environmental protection can become a hobby by becoming our routine. It can be fun shared with friends or relatives. If everyone does at least one little thing, the results will be visible. These 7 days of the challenge below can be just the beginning of your actions. We trust you will do well and look forward to hearing from you. Good luck!

Day 1

Sort out the trash!

Take time to organize and separate your waste into recyclables and non-recyclables, promoting responsible waste disposal and environmental sustainability.



Day 2

Buy bio food for your next week!

Consider making conscientious choices when it comes to purchasing organic food items, supporting sustainable agriculture practices and reducing exposure to harmful pesticides.



Day 3

Use the public transport!

Opt for public transportation over private vehicles to minimize your carbon footprint, contribute to reduced traffic congestion, and promote eco-friendly commuting.



Day 4

Plant a tree!

Contribute to environmental conservation by planting a tree, fostering biodiversity, and aiding in carbon sequestration to combat climate change.



Day 5

Buy reusable bag & bottle!

Make a shift towards eco-friendly alternatives by investing in a reusable bag & water bottle, reducing single-use plastic waste & promoting sustainable living.



Day 6

Clean up your favorite park!

Take part in community care by cleaning up litter and maintaining the cleanliness of your favorite park, fostering a sense of responsibility for shared spaces.



Day 7

Reduce meat consumption!

Contribute to a more sustainable and environmentally friendly lifestyle by reducing meat consumption, which can lower carbon emissions and alleviate the environmental impact of the meat industry.
